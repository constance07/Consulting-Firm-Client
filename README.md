# Monty & Friends LLC — Marketing Site

**Helping businesses improve operations, strengthen margins, and build both short and long-term strategies.**

---
## Project Overview

This is the official marketing website for **Monty & Friends LLC**, a consulting firm that partners with businesses to unlock their full potential. The site is designed to:

- Introduce Sumi Vijay's unqiue and personal approach to business growth: hands-on, people-first consulting
  grounded in structured financial modeling and implementation  carried out directly alongside owners and staff.
- Communicate the firm's core services: Financial Visibility & Modeling, Operational
  Efficiency, Revenue & Pricing Strategy, Capital Planning, and Transition & Growth Support
- Showcase measurable client outcomes: 20% revenue growth, occupancy improvements from 60% to 80%, and a 15% reduction in operating expenses
- Share ongoing financial and operational insights through a blog covering trends that affect business margins, pricing, cost structures, and long-term stability
